Beyond the Resume.

My name is Tyler Nicholas. I am a 17-year-old Seychellois born and raised on the west side of Mahé. I believe that age is not a barrier to impact, and I have spent my early career proving exactly that through business development, community action, and an unwavering pursuit of my goals.

While my ultimate determination is to become a commercial pilot flying the Boeing 777, my drive extends far beyond the flight deck. I am deeply invested in building scalable digital enterprises and ensuring the youth of Seychelles have a respected voice in our national development.

Education

I have attended École Française des Seychelles (EFS) since childhood. I am currently in Première, preparing for the demanding French Baccalaureate (BAC Français & Maths) with specialities in LLCE, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

Aviation

Aviation is my lifelong passion. My absolute determination is to become a commercial pilot. I have already begun building my foundation in the aviation space through managing massive virtual airline operations and digital communities.

Enterprise

As the driving force behind the Tyler Nicholas Group, alongside co-founder Kareem Malhas, I manage and drive growth for multiple subsidiaries, including Falcon LTD and Blackwood Digital Services, delivering real-world digital solutions.

Civic Duty

I serve as a Youth Ambassador, actively working with FoodShare Seychelles and the Tyler Nicholas Foundation. I am currently a candidate for the 21st Cohort of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly to turn youth voices into policy.
